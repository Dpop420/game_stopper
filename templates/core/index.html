How to use

Examples of the utility

General consumer games

General consumer games will have huge file size, and also they needs to install into your PC.
In such a case, you should upload only save game data.

Rogue-like games

Almost Rogue-like games are small file size, and they are not necessary to install. (e.g. Dungeon Crawl, Angband, NetHack, Hengband, Elona, ..)
The good method of that case, you compress the game folder to ZIP, and then you upload whole the compressed file.
